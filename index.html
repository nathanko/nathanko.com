About me

Hello! I'm Nathan, a software developer and current software engineering student at the University of Waterloo. In addition to being a developer and an engineer, however, I'm a designer—using design thinking to find problems that people have and creating tools, interfaces, systems and experiences to improve their lives in some way.

I've built tools and dashboards for the Canadian government that help analysts make sense of heaps of data, enhanced security for phones BlackBerry to protect enterprise data, prototyped the frontend for a new insurance application at Deloitte's Emerging Tech Hub to substantially faster insurance application experience and built web applications at Delego to seamlessly process online payments. Right now, I'm over at Interaptix helping clients use computer vision and augmented reality to modernize their operations.
